## Intra-Abdominal Pressure During Resistance Exercise — Verified March 2026

### Peak IAP by exercise (>80% 1RM, from PMC 6945051 systematic review):
- Squats: >200 mmHg (highest)
- Deadlift: 161-176 mmHg
- Slide row: 164 +/- 39 mmHg
- Leg press: 161 +/- 55 mmHg
- Bench press: 79 +/- 44 mmHg (lowest)

### Diverticular disease considerations:
- Diverticulosis is primarily a function of colonic motility, NOT increased IAP (HealthTap expert opinion)
- However, clinical guidance recommends avoiding breath-holding during heavy exertion with diverticulitis (Diverticulitis.life 2025)
- PMC 11966515 (2025): Patients with symptomatic diverticular disease consumed significantly more animal protein than asymptomatic patients
- Direct evidence linking Valsalva + diverticulosis = complications is WEAK (Level 6 — expert opinion)

### How to apply:
For users with diagnosed diverticular disease doing heavy compounds, recommend checking with gastroenterologist. Not an emergency, but prudent. Flag when coaches don't ask about this.
